Faculty

Dr. Zhengbo Zou is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Civil Engineering at The University of British Columbia. Dr. Zou received his Ph.D. in Civil Engineering from New York University (2020), masters degree in Advanced Infrastructure Systems (2015) from Carnegie Mellon University, and bachelors degree in Civil Engineering (2014) from Tongji University. His current research focuses on advancing the knowledge of automation and intelligence technologies in the life-cycle of design, construction and maintenance of civil infrastructures.
